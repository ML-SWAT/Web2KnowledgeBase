CS 354 Home Page CS/ECE 354 - Machine Organization and Programming 4 credits. An introduction to current system structures of control, communication, memories, processors and I/O devices. Projects involve detailed study and use of a specific small computer hardware and software system. Prerequisites: CS 302 or consent of instructor. Not open to students who have taken CS/ECE 552.
