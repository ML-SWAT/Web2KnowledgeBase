CS302 Course Info Course Information for CS302 Course Description From the Guidebook for Undergraduate Students, May 1994: Construction of algorithms; problem solving; instruction and experience in the use of at least one procedure-oriented language (e.g., Pascal or Fortran); survey of other such languages, advanced programming techniques.
