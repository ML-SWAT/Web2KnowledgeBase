Catherine M. Wagner Catherine M. Wagner Senior Lecturer PhD Cornell University, 1979 My primary responsibilities in the Computer Science Department are in teaching. I am working with others in the department to revise the curriculum for our lower level courses. I am specifically interested in the development of a course for students who are under-prepared for our introductory course in programming.
